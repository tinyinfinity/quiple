Quiple FAQs

Quiple is a daily game that challenges your pattern-matching skills in a fun and engaging way. Every day, you get a new puzzle to solve, keeping your mind sharp and entertained.
I created Quiple as a hands-on way to learn more about artificial intelligence. Rather than just reading about AI, I wanted to build something practical that people could enjoy while I deepened my technical knowledge.
Building Quiple was my introduction to the world of coding. I learned the basics of programming, how to use GitHub for version control, and how to integrate APIs to enhance functionality. Most importantly, I gained an appreciation for iteration and testing—Quiple wasn’t perfect on day one, and refining it over time has been a valuable learning experience.
Beyond the technical side, I also explored the business and marketing aspects of running a digital product. I learned how to create engaging content that keeps users coming back, experimented with different monetization strategies (you’ll notice ads, Amazon affiliate links, and Buy Me a Coffee buttons), and explored ways to expand the Quiple brand with spin-offs like Quiple Sci and Quiple Jr.
